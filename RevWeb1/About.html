About - Revelation Blessings Explored

The Bible is clear. There is a blessing from reading and understanding what is written in the book of revelation.

This Bible Study will look at the book revelation from a blessing point of view. What does it say and how does it bring joy and peace to the reader ? What does it mean when it asked the reader to obey the things written because something is about to happen ?

Revelation 1:3 KJV " Blessed is he that readeth, and they that hear the words of this prophecy, and keep those things which are written therein*: for the time is at hand. "

Revelation 1:3 NLT "God blesses the one who reads the words of this prophecy to the church, and he blesses all who listen to its message and obey what it says, for the time is near."

Revelation 1:3 AMP "Blessed (happy, prosperous, to be admired) is he who reads and those who hear the words of the prophecy, and who keep the things which are written in it [heeding them and taking them to heart]; for the time [of fulfillment] is near."
